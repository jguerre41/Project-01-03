    Recieving Assistance

    Explore the difference in treatment received for substance use and mental health for various age groups with scatter plots.

    Received Substance Use Treatment in the Past Year

    NSDUH measures treatment for alcohol and drug problems, and percentage of treatment services among those who need it.
    Insights: The percentage of people receiving treatment for substances varies significantly across age groups and states, but overall low percentages between 3% and 7%

    Received Mental Health Treatment in the Past Year

    NSDUH tables include data about percentage receiving mental health services among the prevalence of mental health illnesses.
    Insights: The percentage of people receiving mental health treatment is higher than substance use treatment, ranging from about 20% to 40% across age groups and states. It is also visable that younger age groups tend to recieve higher percentages of treatment compared to older groups.
